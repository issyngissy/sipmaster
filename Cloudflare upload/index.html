About Us

Students in Property (SiP) is a student-led committee at Curtin University dedicated to enhancing the university experience for property students.

Our mission is to create meaningful connections between students and industry professionals, providing valuable networking opportunities and insights into the property industry.

Founded by passionate property students, SiP organizes regular events, workshops, and site visits to help members develop professional skills and build relationships that will benefit their future careers.
